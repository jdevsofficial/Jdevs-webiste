01 — Overview

Overview

JDEVS ("we", "us", or "our") is a professional creative design studio based in Nairobi, Kenya. We are committed to protecting the privacy and personal data of everyone who interacts with our business — whether you are a current client, a prospective client, or simply visiting our website.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information in connection with our services, website, and all other points of contact with JDEVS. By using our services or engaging with us in any capacity, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Short version: We only collect what we need, we use it only to serve you, we never sell it, and we keep it secure. You have the right to see, correct, or delete your data at any time.

02 — Data We Collect

Data We Collect

Depending on how you interact with us, we may collect the following categories of personal information:

  • Identity data: Your full name, business or company name, and job title
  • Contact data: Email address, phone number, and physical or postal address
  • Project data: Brief details, design requirements, brand assets, feedback, and any content you supply for your project
  • Financial data: Invoice records and payment references (we do not store card or M-Pesa PIN details)
  • Communication data: Records of messages, emails, and WhatsApp conversations with our team
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device type, and pages visited on our website (collected via analytics tools)
  • Usage data: Information about how you interact with our website, platform, and any client portal

We do not collect or process sensitive personal data such as race, religion, health information, or political opinions unless you choose to share such information as part of a project brief, in which case it is used solely for that purpose.

03 — How We Collect

How We Collect Your Data

We collect personal information through the following channels:

  • When you fill in a contact form, quote request, or onboarding form on our website
  • When you send us an email, WhatsApp message, or reach out via social media
  • When you enter into a project agreement or sign a contract with us
  • When you make a payment or receive an invoice from us
  • When you create an account on our client platform or dashboard
  • Automatically, through cookies and analytics tools when you visit our website
  • When you follow, message, or interact with our social media profiles

We will always aim to be transparent about what we are collecting and why at the point of collection.

04 — How We Use It

How We Use Your Data

We use your personal information only for legitimate business purposes, including:

  • To respond to your enquiries, quote requests, and messages
  • To onboard you as a client and manage your project from start to finish
  • To send you invoices, payment reminders, and receipts
  • To communicate project updates, deliverables, and revision feedback
  • To provide access to our client portal or dashboard where applicable
  • To improve our website, services, and overall client experience
  • To send you occasional updates, offers, or portfolio showcases — only if you have opted in
  • To comply with legal obligations and resolve any disputes
We do not sell your data. Your personal information is never sold, rented, or traded to third parties for marketing or commercial purposes — ever.

05 — Legal Basis

Legal Basis for Processing

We only process your personal data where we have a lawful basis to do so. The legal bases we rely on include:

  • Contract: Processing is necessary to perform a contract with you or to take steps at your request before entering into one
  • Legitimate interests: We may process your data where it is in our legitimate business interests to do so, provided those interests are not overridden by your rights
  • Consent: Where you have given us clear consent, such as opting in to marketing communications
  • Legal obligation: Where we are required to process data to comply with a legal duty

Where we rely on consent as the basis for processing, you have the right to withdraw that consent at any time by contacting us. Withdrawal of consent does not affect the lawfulness of processing carried out prior to withdrawal.

06 — Data Sharing

Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not share your personal data with third parties except in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: Trusted tools we use to run our business, such as Google Workspace for email, payment processors, and project management platforms. These providers are bound by their own privacy policies and are prohibited from using your data for any other purpose
  • Legal compliance: Where we are required by law, court order, or government authority to disclose information
  • Business transfers: In the unlikely event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction, with equivalent protections in place
  • With your consent: In any other situation, only with your explicit written permission
Important: Any third-party links or integrations on our website or platform are governed by those parties' own privacy policies. We are not responsible for their data practices.

07 — Retention

Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected, including for the purposes of satisfying any legal, accounting, or reporting requirements.

  • Active client project data is retained for the duration of the project and up to 3 years after completion
  • Financial and invoice records are retained for a minimum of 7 years in accordance with Kenyan tax law
  • Enquiries that do not result in a project are retained for up to 12 months, then securely deleted
  • Website analytics data is retained in anonymised form for up to 26 months
  • Marketing communication preferences and records are retained until you unsubscribe or request deletion

When your data is no longer required, we will securely delete or anonymise it in a manner that prevents identification or recovery.

08 — Your Rights

Your Rights

Under applicable data protection law, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access: You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification: You may ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to erasure: You may request that we delete your personal data where there is no lawful reason for us to continue holding it
  • Right to restrict processing: You may ask us to pause processing your data in certain circumstances
  • Right to data portability: You may request your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to object: You may object to processing based on legitimate interests or for direct marketing purposes
  • Right to withdraw consent: Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at jdevsdigital2@gmail.com. We will respond to all verifiable requests within 30 days.

09 — Cookies

Cookies

Our website uses cookies — small text files placed on your device — to improve your experience and help us understand how the site is used. The types of cookies we use include:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function correctly. These cannot be disabled
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our site (e.g. pages visited, time spent). We use anonymised data only
  • Preference cookies: Remember your settings and choices to personalise your experience

You can control or disable non-essential cookies through your browser settings at any time. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of certain parts of our website.

We do not use advertising or tracking cookies designed to build profiles for third-party ad networks. Our analytics use is strictly for improving your experience on our site.

10 — Third-Party Links

Third-Party Links & Platforms

Our website, social media profiles, and communications may contain links to third-party websites, platforms, or services — such as Instagram, TikTok, WhatsApp, or payment gateways. These are operated by independent companies and are governed by their own privacy policies.

We have no control over the content, privacy practices, or security of third-party sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policy of any external service before providing your personal information to them. JDEVS accepts no responsibility or liability for third-party privacy practices.

11 — Children

Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for use by businesses and individuals aged 18 years and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 18.

If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a minor, please contact us immediately at jdevsdigital2@gmail.com and we will take prompt steps to delete that information.

12 — Security

Data Security

We take the security of your personal data seriously and implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect it against unauthorised access, loss, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encrypted communication channels (HTTPS, end-to-end encrypted messaging)
  • Secure, password-protected cloud storage with access controls
  • Limited access to personal data — only team members who need it to perform their role
  • Regular reviews of our data handling practices and security posture
Please note: No method of data transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security. In the event of a data breach that affects your rights, we will notify you promptly.

13 — Changes

Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, services, or applicable law. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify existing clients by email.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information. Your continued engagement with JDEVS after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Previous versions of this policy are available on request by contacting us directly.
