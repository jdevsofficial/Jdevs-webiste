04 — Process

Project Process

Our standard project workflow is as follows:

  • Brief & Quotation: You submit your project requirements; we provide a detailed quote and estimated timeline.
  • Deposit & Kick-off: Upon payment of the agreed deposit, work commences and an onboarding form or brief is completed.
  • Initial Concepts: We deliver initial design concepts for your review within the agreed timeframe.
  • Revisions: You provide feedback and we refine the design through a set number of revision rounds as specified in your package.
  • Final Delivery: Upon final approval and receipt of the remaining balance, all final files are delivered to you.

Timelines are estimates and may be affected by client response times, scope changes, or unforeseen circumstances. We will communicate any delays promptly.

Final files are not released until full payment has been received. This includes source files, editable formats, and any licensed assets provided as part of the project.

06 — Revisions

Revisions & Scope of Work

Each project package includes a specified number of revision rounds. A "revision" is defined as changes within the originally agreed scope and direction. The following apply:

  • Revisions must be submitted in a single, consolidated round of feedback — not delivered piecemeal
  • Additional revision rounds beyond the included amount will be charged at our standard hourly rate
  • A complete change of direction, concept, or style after approval of initial concepts constitutes a new scope of work and will be quoted separately
  • Revision requests must be submitted within 14 days of delivery; otherwise, the design is considered approved

Any additions to the original project scope — new deliverables, extra pages, new formats — will be quoted as a separate item and require written approval before work commences.
