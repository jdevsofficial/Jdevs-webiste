About Us

Our journey Designing with Passion.

A creative studio obsessed with brands that resonate, designs that convert, and ideas that leave a mark.

Our Story

From a single Idea to
hundreds of thriving Brands

JDEVS started as a one-person passion project — a sketchbook full of logo concepts and a relentless belief that great design can change the trajectory of a business. Over six years, that passion evolved into a full creative studio trusted by brands across Kenya and beyond.

We have helped launch startups from scratch, reposition established businesses, and give household names a visual identity that commands attention. From the bold rebrand of Rojalin Investments, to the sleek brand system for Tanka Hardware business, to the viral social media presence built for Makom Ventures and Synergy Grid. — every project tells a story of real results, not just pretty pixels.

250+ projects in, our mission remains the same: make your brand impossible to ignore.

Our Journey

Our Story

Six years of growth, one brand-defining project at a time.

2019

The Beginning

JDEVS launched as a freelance logo studio, landing our first 10 clients within 3 months through word of mouth alone.

2020

First Big Brand

Completed a full brand identity for Vertex Foods — packaging, logo, and marketing collateral — our most complex project to date.

2021

Expanding Services

Added web design and social media design to our stack, tripling revenue and crossing 50 completed projects.

2023

150+ Clients Milestone
